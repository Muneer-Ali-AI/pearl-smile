Our Story Is the Story of Every Smile We Created

Since 2009, we have been setting new standards in dentistry in Riyadh — combining world-class medical expertise and exceptional human care to give every patient a smile they can be proud of.

Our Journey

15 Years of Excellence and Innovation

2009

The Beginning — A Dream Becomes Reality

Demo Dental's journey began in Al Olaya, Riyadh, with a small clinic and one doctor carrying an ambitious vision: to provide Riyadh residents with dental care that meets international standards. We opened our doors with three treatment chairs and a firm belief that every patient deserves the best.

2013

Expansion — From Clinic to Comprehensive Center

Our team grew to include 10 doctors from various specialties. We opened specialized departments for orthodontics and pediatric dentistry. Our patient count exceeded 3,000 annually, and we began building our reputation as one of Riyadh's most trusted clinics.

2017

The Digital Revolution — Adopting the Latest Technologies

We were among the first clinics in Riyadh to fully adopt digital dentistry: 3D scanner, digital panoramic X-ray, and computer-guided dental implants. This technological leap improved the precision of our treatments and gave patients better results and a more comfortable experience.

2021

Recognition — International Awards and Accreditations

Our efforts were crowned with the Best Dental Clinic in Riyadh award and ISO 9001 International Accreditation. Our patient count exceeded 8,000 and our team grew to 15 doctors across various subspecialties.

2025

The Present — Continued Leadership and Vision for the Future

Today we are proud of a team of over 20 specialists from top universities in the UK, USA and Germany. We have created over 10,000 happy smiles, and continue investing in the latest dental technologies to remain the first choice for Riyadh residents and visitors.

Our Technologies

We Invest in the Latest Technologies to Serve You

We believe advanced technology is not a luxury — it is a right for every patient who deserves the best results with the least pain and fastest time.

3D Intraoral Scanner

Ultra-precise digital scanning of teeth and gums without uncomfortable traditional impressions. Instant results with accuracy up to 20 microns for digitally designing your perfect smile before treatment begins.

Advanced Digital Panoramic X-Ray

Comprehensive 3D imaging of jaws, teeth, and sinuses with the lowest possible radiation dose. Ultra-clear images enabling precise diagnosis and optimal planning for every case.

Advanced Dental Laser

Gum treatment, teeth whitening, and root canal sterilization with safe laser technology. 80% less pain, twice faster healing, and better results compared to traditional methods.

Central Autoclave Sterilization System

Complete sterilization of all tools and equipment at 134°C under high pressure. We follow infection control protocols approved by the Saudi Ministry of Health and the World Health Organization.

FAQ

Answers to the Most Common Questions About Our Services

How Much Do Dental Implants Cost?

Dental implant costs vary by implant type and jaw bone condition. Prices start from 3,500 SAR per implant including crown. We offer a free consultation where the doctor evaluates your case and provides an accurate cost estimate and comfortable installment options.

Is Teeth Whitening Safe for Enamel?

Yes, teeth whitening at our clinic is completely safe. We use FDA-approved materials and follow scientifically proven protocols to protect enamel. The doctor examines your teeth first to confirm whitening is suitable for your case.

How Long Does Orthodontics Take?

Treatment duration varies by case — on average 6 to 18 months. Invisalign clear aligners can be faster in some cases. The doctor precisely determines the expected duration after examination and digital imaging.

Does Hollywood Smile Require Tooth Filing?

With technological advancement, we can now install ultra-thin veneers (0.3mm) requiring minimal or no filing in some cases. The doctor evaluates your case and chooses the best option to preserve your natural teeth.

Do You Treat Children Under 5?

Yes, we have a specialized pediatric dentistry department equipped to handle all ages starting from 2 years. Our pediatric dentistry specialists are trained in gentle interaction with children and making the visit an enjoyable experience.

Can I Get a Dental Implant on the Same Day as Extraction?

In many cases yes — this is known as immediate implant. The doctor evaluates the bone and tissue condition after extraction and decides on immediate implant feasibility. This option saves you significant time and gives you a temporary tooth in the same session.
